#### Key materials

* Summary
  * Ogmios and Kupo readiness continues to be the biggest challenge for the hard fork progress
    * Ogmios should be added to the critical tooling list
    * It should be pointed out in any comms mentioning that Ogmios isnt ready yet, that its the private project
    * Main issue from the Ogmios team perspective is that the impact is big due to the fact that the node version 11.0.1, which is meant for van Rossem, also contains some feature of the future hard fork/era Dijkstra
    * IO team members John and Erik created massive PRs in draft trying to unblock progress even though its not their codebase
      * Matthias from the Ogmios team is participating as much as possible giving his guidance, reviews and technical expertise but time availability continues to be the issue
    * There are at least two possible approaches
      * Continue to work on the PRs in their current state trying to get them over the line and in the position to be merged or
      * Create PRs with the reduced scope to focus on the van Rossem readiness support and keeing Dijkstra features support as little as needed
    * It was decided that IO team should try to get additional resources in reviewing the work being done by John and Erik and giving it potentially the last needed push
      * There needs to be a rapid progress made in less than 2 days, before the next hard fork working group meeting on 21st May (which is also a preprod epoch boundary)
      * That is supposed to be the last possible date when the hard fork working group can recommend forking Cardano PreProd testnet (governance action ratification would be enacted on 21st and enacted 5 days later on 26th of May). The governance action submission date for mainnet would stay the same, **29th May**.
        * The criteria is relatively simple, Ogmios PRs is raised and official
      * If there is no Ogmios PR raised before the next PreProd epoch boundary on 21st, hard fork is highly likely to be delayed by at least one epoch, possibly more (depends on the iterative development work being done on the PR itself)
  * Exchanges need Rosetta-Java (expected by the end of next week) to be ready in order to progress on readiness, and Rosetta-Java is dependent on Yaci Store which is expected to become ready by the end of this week
    * All exchanges have been contacted
  * Overall readiness steadily increases
    * More ecosystem tooling becomes ready each day
    * SPO adoption rate of the hard fork ready node 11.0.1 is growing and is now at 32% according to pooltool.io (more than 10% increase in the last 5 days). This sets it as the version with the biggest single share.
  * Cardano entities (IO, Cardano Foundation, Midnight, Emurgo and Intersect) have established comms weeklies on Monday and also agreed communication channels for better collaboration and propagation of van Rossem related comms
  * Risk log was updated with increased likelihood for <mark style="color:$warning;">`Community is not ready for the Hard Fork`</mark>, <mark style="color:$warning;">`Failure to Hard Fork in a Timely Manner`</mark> and <mark style="color:$warning;">`Ogmios upgrade not ready`</mark> risks
