#### Key materials

* Summary
  * DBSync issue from 20th May, with inflated values being written to epoch.out\_sum and epoch.fees at the first epoch boundary following a restart, rollback, cache miss, or upgrade, have been addressed - blog available [here](https://intersectmbo.org/news/notice-epoch-aggregate-values-issue-in-db-sync-13.7.0.x)
    * There is a new version of DBSync, [13.7.1.0](https://github.com/IntersectMBO/cardano-db-sync/releases/tag/13.7.1.0), that solves it properly without the workaround needed
    * Its recommended to upgrade to 13.7.1.0
      * If you are running 13.7.0.5, that version is also van Rossem compatible, and it just needs migration script executed as described in the release notes
  * Ogmios PR hasnt been raised yet, and having in mind its considered to be the critial tool, hard fork working group didnt recommend ratification and subsequently enactment of the hard fork initiation governance action on Cardano PreProd testnet. However, hard fork working group also discussed few different paths ahead with most prominent ones to be
  * Wait for Ogmios author to review PR changes
    * ideally to happen this week but
    * more likely to happen mid June given time availability
  * Fork Ogmios, make it useful in the van Rossem context, and give Ogmios author some time to assess changes from his perspective and merge the into the original code potentially
  * The decision will be made before or during the next hard fork working group meeting on Thursday, 28th May
  * Should there be a viable Ogmios PR in sight on the next meeting, hard fork working group will consider recommending ratifying hard fork on PreProd and also sticking to the original plan and submission of the hard fork initiation governance action on mainnet this week, 29th May
  * That also means that the submission of the hard fork initiation governance action on mainnet would happen few days earlier than the van Rossem hard fork enactment on PreProd&#x20;
  * Risk log is to be updated:
    * With the latest findings on Ogmios readiness
    * With the DBSync issue from 20th May as accepted risk
    * With the casing on the plutus built-ins
  * Plutus Cost model parameter update governance action is expected to be submitted on mainnet later today, 27th May
  * Hard Fork working group will continue to assess van Rossem upgrade readiness every day, in and out of the scheduled working group meetings
