#### Key materials

* Summary
  * Latest version of DBSync is [13.7.1.0](https://github.com/IntersectMBO/cardano-db-sync/releases/tag/13.7.1.0) and its recommended to upgrade to that version
    * If you are running DBSync version 13.7.0.5, it is also van Rossem compatible, and it just needs migration script executed as described in the release notes
  * Ogmios is forked, will be made van Rossem compatible shortly through the respective transparent PR, and it will give much needed time to Ogmios author to assess changes from his perspective and merge the into the original code for the mainnet fork enactment moment
    * Respective PR wont change any of the frontend, its meant to provide the service that communicates with the node
    * It is reasonable to reintegrate and retest once the Ogmios van Rossem compatible changes are merged in the original repo
  * Kupo is close to be done and ready, possibly even today
  * Based on the above Hard Fork Working Group recommends without any voiced or written objections:
    * Forking the PreProd wtih ratification happening on the next epoch boundary on 31st May and enactment an epoch later, on 5th June
    * Go/No go decision meeting for the submission of the hard fork initiation governance action to be held on 8th June
      * Should Hard Fork Working Group decide on 8th June that its a "go", hard fork initiation governance action will be submitted on the same day, 8th June
  * Plutus Cost Model parameter update governance action, submitted on 26th May, is already gaining positive traction 15.59% off yes votes
    * mincommitteesize parameter update governance action, chained to the plutus cost model one, is expected to be submitted on mainnet early next week
  * Readiness tracker is updated with more tools, libraries, indexers, SPOs becoming ready
  * Risk log was assessed and adjusted accordingly in the light of the discussion on todays call and is showing that the effective risk management strategies are already yielding results
